#10x Genomics count page: https://www.10xgenomics.com/support/software/cell-ranger/latest/analysis/running-pipelines/cr-gex-count
#from 10x Genomics website: The cellranger count takes FASTQ files and performs alignment, filtering, barcode counting, and UMI counting. It uses the 10x Barcodes to generate feature-barcode matrices, determine clusters, and perform gene expression analysis.
